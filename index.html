Sonoma County Bunfest

A day of fun, learning, and (of course!) absolute cuteness!

Sunday, April 29, 2018 ~ from 11 a.m. until 3 p.m.

Burton Avenue Recreation Center, Rohnert Park

More info below!

Free admission and free parking!

An afternoon all about bunnies

Join us at the second annual Sonoma County Bunfest, celebrating bunnies and their friends with educational speakers and local rescue organizations. And you won't want to miss the latest in bunny toys or delicious food from our vendors!

  • Meet adoptable bunnies
  • Get tips about how to have a happy house rabbit
  • Learn about medical issues and special care
  • Shop for toys and supplies at the Bunny Boutique
