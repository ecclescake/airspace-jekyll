Talks

RHDV2 update: Learn about the new deadly rabbit virus

Dr Anthony A. Pilny

Veterinary Advisor for the House Rabbit Society and member of the RHDV2 Task Force

Dr. Pilny has lectured nationally and internationally about avian and exotic pet medicine and has published numerous scientific articles and book chapters on birds, rabbits, and other exotic species. Dr. Pilny is a member of the Association of Avian Veterinarians, Association of Exotic Mammal Veterinarians, Arizona Veterinary Medical Association, and the American Veterinary Medical Association. He currently serves as the consulting medical director of the House Rabbit Society and is a member of the RHDV2 task force. He also serves on the advisory boards of The Avian Welfare Coalition and One Earth Conservation and chair of the Welfare Committee for the Association of Avian Veterinarians.

Dr. Pilny currently works at Arizona Exotic Animal Hospital in Phoenix, Arizona and has devoted the last 19 years to exotic pet veterinary practice with a focus on animal welfare.

10 Surprising Facts About Rabbits

Mark Hawthorne

Author of The Way of The Rabbit

Independent and resourceful, rabbits represent balance, rebirth, speed, fertility, resurrection, abundance, creativity, magic, and harmony. Yet they are much more than symbols—they are unique individuals with complex inner lives. After spending two years researching his new book, The Way of the Rabbit, author Mark Hawthorne will share 10 of the most surprising things he learned about these remarkable animals.

Learn about Mark

When to Go to the Vet

Anne Martin, PhD

Executive Director, House Rabbit Society

Domestic rabbits are companion animals and should be afforded at least the same individual rights, level of care, and opportunity for longevity as commonly afforded to dogs and cats who live as human companions.

Learn about HRS

Bunny Basics; Selecting the Right Rabbit and Basic Care

Marcy Berman

Founder and Executive Director, SaveABunny, Inc.

Marcy Berman is the Founder and Executive Director of SaveABunny.org, an award-winning non-profit rabbit rescue group based in Mill Valley, CA. After falling under the spell of a spirited stray rabbit she found in 1999, Marcy left a corporate marketing and advertising career to focus on advocating for and raising awareness of rabbits as loving, intelligent and beloved indoor companion animals. Appalled by the near 100% euthaniasia rates of rabbits at many shelters, Marcy created SaveABunny to be a last chance rescue and healing sanctuary for those rabbits in most urgent need.

Learn about SaveABunny

Caring for Baby Rabbits

Dr Hilary Stern

Founder and Owner of the Exotic Pet Clinic of Santa Cruz

Hilary Stern DVM DABVP is the founder and owner of the Exotic Pet Clinic of Santa Cruz, an animal hospital devoted exclusively to the care of exotic pets.

Dr. Stern has worked for a number of exotic pet practices in the Bay Area. She is a consulting veterinarian for The Rabbit Haven, Native Animal Rescue, and Mickaboo Companion Bird Rescue. She is also the surgeon at low-cost spay and neuter clinics for rabbits in Santa Cruz County.

Dr. Stern is board certified in Exotic Companion Mammal Practice by the American Board of Veterinary Practitioners. She is on the Marketing Committee of the Association of Exotic Mammal Veterinarians, and the Education Committee for the Association of Avian Veterinarians. She has presented at exotic pet conferences, and has published in the Journal of Exotic Pet Medicine.
