Sonoma County Bunfest

A day of fun, learning, and (of course!) absolute cuteness!

Saturday, March 30, 2019 ~ from 11 a.m. until 3 p.m.

Rohnert Park Community Center, Rohnert Park

More info below!

Free admission and free parking!

An afternoon all about bunnies

Join us at the third annual Sonoma County Bunfest, celebrating bunnies and their friends with educational speakers and local rescue organizations. And you won't want to miss the latest in bunny toys or delicious food from our vendors!

NEW ~ Kids' activity center ~ plant a bunny herb garden, face painting, and more!

  • Meet adoptable bunnies—shelters and rescue groups will be on site
  • Educational speakers—learn about bunny care and more
  • Tasty lunch treats will be available for purchase from Muir's Tea Room
  • Shop for fresh hay, toys, treats, and beds
  • Photo ops with costumed rabbit
  • Great raffle prizes
